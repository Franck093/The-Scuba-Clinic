We keep your dive gear healthy

The Scuba Clinic is South Australia's only dedicated service center. We are qualified to service all brands of equipment including light commercial. We always hold up to date manufacturer's certifications. We will service all types of equipment, regardless of age, subject to availability of parts.



Your service technician, Ian Wolverson has extensive experience and knowledge of the following brands:





Aeris      Apollo      Apeks      Aqualung      Atomic      Boss      Cressi      Dacor      Dive Rite      Edge      Genesis      Halcyon      Hog      Hollis      Interspiro (AGA)      IST      Mares      Mirage      Oceanic      Performance Scuba      Poseidon      Rebel      Sea Hornet      Seac Sub      SeaQuest      Scubapro      Shark Shield      Sherwood      Sub Gear      Tusa      US Divers      Uwatec      XS Scuba      Zeagle     



Our service includes:


  • Compete dismantling of all components and inspection for abnormal wear or damage
  • Ultrasonic cleaning using approved fluids
  • Only genuine parts fitted, as per manufacturer's recommendations
  • Approved lubricants used (no silicone)
  • Professional tuning of all second stages on a Magnehelic test gauge
  • In water final quality control checks
  • All old parts returned to customer
  • Professional service report sheets supplied
  • Prompt turnaround time, even during summer months
  • All service work covered by a minimum 90 day warranty




We also offer:


  • Same day service for visiting regional clients
  • Pre-service quotes, if required
  • Dive computer battery change
  • Assembly and testing of new equipment purchased via internet



We welcome regional / interstate customers, please email or phone to ask about our freight deal !
